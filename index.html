Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Cashews (Kaju)
  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Ghee (Clarified Butter)

Recipe

  1. Prepare Cashew Powder
  2. Make Sugar Syrup
  3. Mix Cashew Powder
  4. Cool and Knead
  5. Roll Out the Dough
  6. Cut into Diamond Shapes
  7. Add Final Touch

Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • Milk - 1 liter
  • Lemon Juice or Vinegar
  • Water - 4 cups (for the sugar syrup)
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Cardamom Powder (optional)

Recipe

  1. Boil Milk
  2. Curdle Milk with Lemon Juice or Vinegar
  3. Strain and Rinse the Curdled Milk
  4. Knead the Chhena (Cottage Cheese)
  5. Shape into Small Balls
  6. Prepare Sugar Syrup
  7. Cook the Balls in Sugar Syrup
  8. Cool and Serve
